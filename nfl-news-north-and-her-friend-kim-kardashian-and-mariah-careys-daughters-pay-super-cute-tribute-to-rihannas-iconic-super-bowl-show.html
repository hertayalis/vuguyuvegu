North and Her Friend: Kim Kardashian and Mariah Careys Daughters Pay SuperCute Tribute

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, May 17, 2024

The Super Bowl LVII was a memorable night. In addition to the Kansas-Philadelphia showdown, people were eagerly looking forward to the halftime show. The Kansas City Chiefs defeated the National Football Conference champion Philadelphia Eagles in the final moments while Rihanna went with another notable event to her already stellar career. Among many avid Rihanna fans all over the globe, two went on to dedicate a unique recreation of the halftime show.

Recently, a video of ‘Keeping up with the Kardashians’ star Kim Kardashian’s daughter, North West, teaming up with five-time Grammy Award winner Mariah Carey’s daughter went viral.

A cute replica of the Super Bowl Show

Rihanna has always been looking up to those who have ‘done it’ before her. Ever since her younger days she had identified her passion and had ideal role models to mimic.

One of her idols was the legend, Mariah Carey, “I loved Mariah Carey, Celine Dion, Bob Marley. I would watch their videos all day long on TV because that’s what they would play in the Caribbean. I would start mimicking everything I heard, everything Mariah did, I would try to do.”

Ironically, Mariah Carey’s daughter, just like many in her generation, has the same admiration for Rihanna. The star singer’s daughter Monroe Cannon went on to recreate Rihanna’s Super Bowl performance.

However, she was not alone, as Monroe Cannon teamed up with another notable celebrities daughter. Kim Kardashian’s daughter could also be seen in the video sharing a joyous moment with Carey’s daughter.

Kim Kardashian and North West have been Rihanna fans for quite a while now, as the mother-daughter duo went on to recreate another instance of the Super Bowl show back in February.

 An insane revelation

Rihanna’s Super Bowl halftime show marked her return to the music world after five long years. While fans have been eagerly waiting for a new album, her growing baby bump however convinced fans of her priorities at the time.

Her all-red ensemble was an endearing homage to her friend and fashion great André Leon Talley. RiRi went on to rejuvenate her fans, as her music experienced a 211% increase in on-demand streams and a 390% increase in digital song sales overall.

Throughout her career, Rihanna has established herself as one of the greats, winning 9 Grammy Awards, 12 Billboard Music Awards, and 13 American Music Awards, leaving behind a legacy to yearn for.
