Kate Middleton Shares Her Children's Favorite Foods

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, August 28, 2024
  • Duchess Kate spoke with Mary Berry on the importance of cooking at home as a family during last night's A Berry Royal Christmas special.
  • During the special, Kate shared two foods that Princess Charlotte and Prince Louis can't get enough of.

During BBC’s A Berry Royal Christmas special, the Duchess of Cambridge gave a sneak peek at some of Prince Louis’s and Princess Charlotte’s favorite foods.

While touring the Wisley Royal Horticultural Society garden, Berry asked the duchess which foods and treats her children enjoy. Kate endearingly quipped that Louis loves beetroot and Charlotte is, of course, a fan of Charlotte potatoes.

“I really enjoy [cooking at home] and again for them to be creative, for them to try to be as independent as possible with it,” said Kate about encouraging her children to be participants in the kitchen.

Charlotte potatoes are essential in British cuisine, known for their creamy skins, light yellow flesh, and smaller size. They have a bright, fresh flavor that allows them to be delicious hot or cold; ideally, they’re roasted or boiled for potato salad.

Louis’s preference for beetroot inspired Berry to prepare a decadent chocolate beetroot cake during the special in honor of the little prince. She explained to viewers that shredded beetroot when baked within a cake batter takes on a similar texture to carrot cake—moist and delicious.

Berry contributed her signature baked goods to Kate’s Back to Nature event in September, when the duchess highlighted the importance of children spending time outside, exploring nature, and remaining active.
