Shoppers Say They Look 15 Younger With This $17 Neutrogena Eye Cream

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, June 28, 2024

For a long time, I was one of those people who saw eye creams as clever marketing. “Ah yes, one more item my beauty routine definitely needs,” I’d scoff as I walked through the drugstore. But then I tested one and, within three weeks, I was changing my tune. Formulated specifically for the puffy, darkened, thin skin under my eyes, I (begrudgingly) started seeing a difference. Now, not only do I never go to bed without applying one, but I’m always on the lookout for ones shoppers swear by, one of which just got majorly marked down on Amazon for Black Friday.

Right now, Neutrogena’s Rapid Wrinkle Repair Eye Cream is 42 percent off at Amazon — now just $17. According to shoppers, the retinol-infused under-eye treatment made them look 15 years younger in just one month.

This on-sale eye cream is part of the drugstore brand’s Rapid Wrinkle Repair line, which has been a staple in Jennifer Garner’s skincare routine, calling the accompanying oil one of her “favorites.” Like her go-to oil, this eye cream is also formulated with retinol — which reduces the appearance of fine lines, wrinkles, and dark spots — in addition to hydrating hyaluronic acid and glycerin.

Shoppers rave that it “immediately hydrates [their] under-eyes [while] reducing the appearance of fine lines.” “This eliminated most of my under-eye wrinkles, crows feet, and under-eye dark circles,” wrote one fan, with a shopper in their 40s agreeing, saying that their and dark circles “have gone away” and the “skin around [their] eyes is a lot brighter.” And a 50-year-old customer explained that they can’t go without the eye cream, writing that when they use it, “people seriously think [they] are in [their] late 30s or early 40s.” 

And thanks to those smoothing effects, customers say it changes how they apply makeup. “My eyeliner glided on instead of stopping and starting, due to puffiness,” they wrote, adding that their eyelids were “smoother” in just 24 hours.

While retinol might be a scary thought to those with reactionary skin (been there, gotten over that), even shoppers with “crazy sensitive skin” note that they're happy with their results. And according to a customer who experienced negative reactions to other eye creams, they’ll never use anything but Neutrogena’s Rapid Wrinkle Repair cream again. “This is fantastic for my super sensitive skin…my eyes are brighter and skin is softer.”

For brighter, younger eyes, make sure to grab Neutrogena’s Rapid Wrinkle Repair Eye Cream while it’s 42 percent off during Amazon's Black Friday sale (I know I will be).
