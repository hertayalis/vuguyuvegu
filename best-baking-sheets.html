The 3 Best Baking Sheets of 2024

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, May 17, 2024

We tested and reviewed the best baking sheets to help you pick the right ones for your kitchen. Use them to bake cookies, roast veggies, and more!

Baking sheets are some of the most-used pans in my kitchen. I reach for them whenever I want to make roasted vegetables or bake chocolate chip cookies. I pull out a sheet pan when I’m whipping up a homemade pizza or cooking off crispy potato wedges. And when I want to make the easiest possible dinner, I grab a baking sheet to cook this tasty sheet pan gnocchi.

Given all that baking sheets can do, I think that everyone should own a good one. I recently tested and reviewed the best baking sheets to help you choose the right one for your kitchen. Luckily, these workhorse pans are durable and affordable. Invest a little in a great baking sheet now, and it will serve you well for years!

    I’ve been using the Nordic Ware Naturals Half Sheet Pan for years, and when I tested it alongside the other pans here, it still came out on top.

I recommend lining this pan with parchment paper or a silicone baking mat when making baked goods. When you’re roasting, it works great with or without parchment!

    3. Best Nonstick Baking Sheet: OXO Good Grips Non-Stick Pro Half Sheet

    If you’re looking for the best baking sheets with a non-stick surface, you have two options:

  • Line one of the above aluminum pans with parchment paper. This works perfectly for baking delicate cookies, and cleanup is a breeze!
  • Buy a baking sheet with a non-stick coating.

    • If you want to go the second route, I recommend the OXO Good Grips Non-Stick Half Sheet Pan. It’s made of heavy gauge aluminized steel with a ceramic reinforced non-stick surface. The Chicago Metallic Non-Stick Baking Sheet is available at Amazon for $17.99.

    USA Pan Bakeware Half Sheet Pan

    I’ve had a number of USA Pan cookie sheets in the past, and I’ve really liked them. Similar to the OXO non-stick pan, the USA Pan Half Sheet Pan is made of aluminized steel with a corrugated surface to promote air circulation. Reinforced rims provide a sturdy grip and diminish warping. I didn’t include this pan among my top choices because the nonstick coating wears off over time. The USA Pan Half Sheet Pan is available at Amazon for $23.99.

    Vollrath Wear-Ever Half-Size Sheet Pan

    Like my top 2 picks, this sheet pan is made of uncoated aluminum to promote even baking and browning. However, I can’t recommend this pan because the open rim has sharp edges. I worried about cutting myself while handling or cleaning it. The Vollrath Wear-Ever Sheet Pan is available at Amazon for $29.87.

    How We Picked and Tested

    I selected baking sheets to test based on online reviews, personal recommendations, and my past experiences with different baking sheets as a recipe developer. I narrowed my list to six rimmed half sheet pans because

  • The 18 x 13-inch size is practical. You can fit an entire sheet pan dinner or a big batch of roasted vegetables or cookies on a half sheet pan. If you have a standard-size oven, this is the pan size I would recommend.
  • The rim has benefits. It captures juices and oils released by whatever you’re cooking and gives the pan structure.

    • I roasted a mix of sweet potatoes, cauliflower, and broccolini on each selection, and I also baked a batch of my vegan chocolate chip cookies. I evaluated the vegetables for speed and evenness of browning and noted whether or not they stuck to the baking sheets. When baking the cookies, I looked to see if they stuck to nonstick pans (I recommend parchment for uncoated pans) and if they cooked evenly without burning underneath.

    My top picks yielded consistent results—vegetables with crisp, golden brown edges and perfectly baked cookies—without warping every time.
