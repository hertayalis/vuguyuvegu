About

Canadian actor best known for portraying the Blue/Red S.P.D. Ranger in Power Rangers S.P.D. He has made guest appearances on notable shows such as Flashpoint, Nikita, and Degrassi: The Next Generation.

Before Fame

Before landing his main cast role on Power Rangers, he played Gym bunny in an episode of Queer as Folk, Monty in an episode of Show Me Yours, and Mike Jacobs in an episode of Wild Card.
