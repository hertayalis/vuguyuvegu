Rick Hall – Biography

Rick Hall was born in Mississippi on January 31, 1932. Record producer and music publisher who was nominated as Producer of the Year by the Grammys in 1970.

A year later, he was honored as Billboard’s Producer of the Year for the World in 1971. He produced music for Donny Osmond among numerous others. He was in an R&B band called The Fairlanes and was a successful songwriter in the ’50s.
