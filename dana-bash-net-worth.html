Dana Bash’s Background

Dana Bash was born in June of 1971 in Manhattan, New York City. Her parents were Stuart, a producer at ABC News, and Frances, an author and educator in the field of Jewish studies. She spent time living in New Jersey and Washington, DC during her childhood. Bash attended Pascack Hills High School in Montvale, New Jersey, and graduated cum laude with a bachelor’s degree in political communications from George Washington University. She completed internships at CBS, NBC, and CNN while studying.

Dana Bash’s Career Highlights

Dana Bash began her career at CNN as a producer after graduating from college. She produced shows such as “Late Edition,” “Evans & Novak,” and “Inside Politics.” Her work covering the United States Senate led to her becoming CNN’s chief congressional correspondent.

Bash has conducted key interviews with political figures such as Senator Rob Portman, Representative Gabrielle Giffords, and Senators Rand Paul and Ted Cruz. She has reported on major stories, including Hurricane Katrina and the capture of Saddam Hussein.

Bash has covered presidential primaries and debates for CNN, as well as midterm elections. She hosted the 2019 Democratic presidential primary debate and anchored special coverage of the 2020 election leading up to Joe Biden’s victory. In 2021, she joined fellow anchor Jake Tapper as a co-host of the CNN Sunday morning program “State of the Union.”

Honors and Awards

Dana Bash has received several honors and awards for her journalism work. In 2012, she was part of the duo that won the Joan S. Barone Award for excellence in congressional reporting. Later in 2019, she won the National Press Foundation’s Sol Taishoff Award and also won their Everett McKinley Dirksen Award three times. Additionally, she received an honorary doctoral degree from Franklin Pierce University in Rindge, New Hampshire.

Bash got hitched to fellow CNN news anchor John King in 2008, and the pair welcomed their son Jonah. However, they divorced in 2012. Bash then dated Spencer Garrett, an actor and painter based in Los Angeles.
