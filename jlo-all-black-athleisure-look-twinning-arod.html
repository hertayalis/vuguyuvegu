J.Lo Dons Black Outfit While House Hunting with Alex Rodriguez

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, July 21, 2024
  • Jennifer Lopez matched with fiancé Alex Rodriguez while out house hunting in Malibu over the weekend.
  • Lopez donned a sleek black crop top with matching high-rise leggings and platform sneakers for the excursion.
  • The couple recently participated in a Black Lives Matter protest in Los Angeles following nationwide outcry over the state of police brutality in the United States.

Jennifer Lopez made another case for chic athleisure over the weekend while house hunting in Malibu with her fiancé, Alex Rodriguez, per Just Jared.

The Hustlers star coordinated with her fiancé in matching all black, opting for her signature sartorial ensemble of a form-fitting crop top by Naked Wardrobe, high-rise leggings, and her favorite all-white Kooples platform sneakers. Lopez wore her hair in wavy tresses and accessorized the look with oversize black sunglasses, while Rodriguez donned a sleek black crew-neck shirt and matching black denim.

The couple's public outing comes days after the pair participated in a Black Lives Matter protest in Los Angeles. Lopez revealed on Instagram that her twins, Max and Emme Muñiz, helped her make the signs that she and Rodriguez carried while demonstrating.

"We talked about how if one person doesn’t have justice then no one does. That this country was built on the belief of freedom and justice for all," revealed Lopez on Instagram alongside a video showcasing the massive size of the L.A. protest. "We must take a stand for what we believe in and fight against the injustices in this world. So we continue to peacefully protest until there is change."
