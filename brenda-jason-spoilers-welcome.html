Brenda &amp; Jason (Spoilers Welcome) - General Hospital

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, May 26, 2024

Okay I know this might seem far from left field and JASAM fans are going to want to hang me but I always thought what it would be like if Brenda and Jason got togheter. Given I know their history that they “can’t stand” eachother and they are frenemies. They have been married for convience a couple of times together but no love. I would not mind TPTB exploring a “real” relationship with them. I know Jason is annoyed by Brenda and they have NEVER been “friends” like he in Carly. Jason just has always had this feeling to protect Brenda as do ALL the men in her life Jax & Sonny included. She is mesmerizing but Jason has NEVER fallen under her love spell. I wonder how the TPTB would play this out. I know it would be a great storyline and a lot of possibilities. I wonder what Sonny would make of this? It would be unexpected for everyone because not even most fans could fathom this. But I have always been a fan of Brenda and Jason and have always wondered “What if”. What if they grew to be more than frenemies? What if they fell in love? How would this change the canvas of GH? What would this mean for Sonny and Brenda & Jason? I would love for this to be explored. I do understand that Brenda unlike Carly and Sam is the only woman who hasn’t “been with” sexually Sonny, Jason, and Jax so I could see how this could be a turn-off for the developing relationship that Brenda and Jason could have. I want to see it happen though cause I never even thought it was something TPTB would do, but ALWAYS WISHED AND HOPED FOR IT!!!
