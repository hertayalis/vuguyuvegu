Serena Williams shares video getting her eyebrows waxed after Wimbledon

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, July 25, 2024

After winning her 36th grand slam title at Wimbledon on Saturday at the age of 33, Serena Williams amazed the world with her enduring athleticism.

But after celebrating her victory with her celebrity friends and family, it was business as usual for the tennis ace, who headed to her local beauty salon to get her eyebrows trimmed.

The star, who beat Garbine Muguruza in this weekend's impressive display, has shared a video live from the salon on her Instagram page. 

Scroll down for video 

Wimbledon champion Serena Williams has shared a video live from the salon on her Instagram page with the caption 'Lol finally getting them shaped! Hahahha #haters I love you!!! Hahah but I still like them all natural! But for now you win lol' 

The video, which shows a beautician shearing her brows with scissors before grooming them, is captioned: 'Lol finally getting them shaped! Hahahha #haters I love you!!! Hahah but I still like them all natural! But for now you win lol' [sic].

The video, which Serena posted to her 1.5m followers, now has nearly 25,000 'likes' and over 2,000 comments.
