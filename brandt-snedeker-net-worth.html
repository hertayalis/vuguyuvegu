Brandt Snedeker Net Worth

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, July 24, 2024

Brandt Snedeker, born on December 8, 1980, in Nashville, Tennessee, is a professional golfer. One of his most notable achievements is winning the 2012 FedEx Cup and the AT&T Pebble Beach National Pro-Am twice, in 2013 and 2015. He is currently ranked in the top 25 on the official World Golf Ranking list.

According to sources, Brandt Snedeker’s net worth is estimated to be over $20 million as of early 2017. His wealth has been amassed through his professional golf career, which began in 2004.

Brandt, the younger of two sons of Candice and Larry Snedeker, was introduced to golf by his grandmother. He attended Vanderbilt University and graduated in 2003 with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Communication. Before turning professional, Snedeker won the US Amateur Public Links Championship in 2003.

Between 2004 and 2007, Snedeker recorded two wins at the Nationwide Tour and earned a total of nearly $550,000, laying the groundwork for his current net worth.

In 2007, he joined the PGA Tour and was named the PGA Tour Rookie of the Year that same year. His participation in various tournaments helped him earn his place in the Top 100 on the World Golf Ranking list and significantly increased his overall wealth.

His career breakthrough came in 2012 when he won his first PGA Tour – the Farmers Insurance Open at Torrey Pines golf course in San Diego, California, and later won the FedEx Cup and Tour Championship. These achievements added $10 million to his net worth.

In 2013, Snedeker reached his career-high ranking of 4th in the World Golf Rankings and continued to achieve success in various tournaments, further bolstering his net worth. He also won the RBC Canadian Open and represented the US national team at the Presidents Cup.

In 2015, he won another AT&T Pebble Beach National Pro-Am tournament title and continued to perform well in other tournaments, contributing to his overall wealth.

Brandt Snedeker has been married to Mandy since 2008, and they have a daughter and a son. The family resides in Nashville, Tennessee.
