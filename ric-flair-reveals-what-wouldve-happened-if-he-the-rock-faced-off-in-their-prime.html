Ric Flair Reveals What Wouldve Happened If He &amp; The Rock Faced Off In Their Prime

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, September 7, 2024

Two-time WWE Hall Of Famer Ric Flair is a bona fide legend in the world of pro wrestling, having competed in several promotions in his career.

The 16-time World Champion is currently signed to WWE and makes periodic appearances on WWE television. He will be making an appearance on RAW Legends Night on January 4th, 2021.

Flair recently appeared on Kevin Hart’s Cold As Balls on YouTube, where Hart asked Flair about The Rock.

The Great One is a good friend to both Ric Flair and Kevin Hart, so this question was certainly not unexpected. The Nature Boy gave a lot of praise to The Rock and revealed what would have happened if the two squared off against each other in their prime.

“They would have had to build new arenas. “

Flair was also asked if he and The Rock share any similarities. He stated that they do have similar energy.

“When I see him, he is so damn entertaining. He’s got skills, where he can sing where he can play instruments. I didn’t have that, but I had the same energy.”

Flair had faced off against The Rock on the July 29th, 2002 episode of Monday Night RAW, but Flair was certainly not in his prime at the time.
