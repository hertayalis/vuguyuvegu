Ella Bands Biography

Bands was born on 23rd January 1999 in Bronx, New York, the United States of America. Ella’s parents are of Cuban and African-American descent and her real name is Ella Rodriguez. The social media influencer also has a sibling named Mella Barbie.

How Old Is Ella Bands?

What Does Ella Bands Do?

As already revealed, Ella is a social media influencer and entrepreneur. She has a verified Instagram account with over 2 million followers. Her YouTube channel has followers too however, she is very active on Instagram where she shares her profession.

Who Is Ella Bands Dating?

The American celebrity was in a relationship with singer Boogie Wit Da Hoodie. The two were together before parting ways. They had two children together namely Melody Valentine (born on 14th February 2017) and Artist Julius Dubose Jr. (born on 27 June 2020).

Who Is Ella Bands’ ex-boyfriend, Boogie Wit Da Hoodie?

Boogie Wit da Hoodie, also known as Artist Julius Dubose, is a rapper, singer, and songwriter from New York City. He first gained popularity with his 2016 debut mixtape, “Artist,” which featured the hit single “My Shit.”

Since then, Boogie has released several successful projects, including his debut studio album “The Bigger Artist,” which hit #2 on the Billboard 200 chart, and its follow-up, “Hoodie SZN,” which became the first album to top the chart with all of its tracks being classified as “album cuts,” rather than traditional singles.

Boogie’s music is known for its catchy hooks and introspective lyrics, often exploring themes of heartbreak, relationships, and personal growth. In addition to his solo work, he has collaborated with a number of other artists, including 21 Savage, PnB Rock, and Post Malone.
