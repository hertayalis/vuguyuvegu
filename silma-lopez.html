Meet Silma López Who Played Lola in “Valeria”

Silma López (age: 31 years; born on July 3, 1991) is a Spanish actor whose versatility as an artist is evident in her diverse roles. She has made notable appearances in popular television shows such as “Centro médico,” “Private Sales,” and “Cosas de la vida.”

In addition to her television success, López has also made her mark in cinema. She has worked on several noteworthy movies, including “8 Dates,” “Our Last Weekend,” “Good Night, Said Miss Bird,” “Cuento de Verano,” and “Get Her… If You Can.”

These films have provided her with the opportunity to collaborate with acclaimed directors and showcase her skills in different genres, from romantic comedies to horror thrillers.

One notable project in Silma’s filmography is the 2022 Jacobo Martínez-directed horror-thriller, “13 Exorcisms,” highlighting her versatility and commitment to challenging roles.

However, it was in 2020 that López truly gained widespread fame and recognition with her role as Lola in the Netflix series “Valeria.”

Directed by Inma Torrente, this Spanish show follows the story of Valeria, a writer who finds herself at a creative and personal crossroads. As Valeria navigates the challenges of her career and marriage, she seeks solace and support from her three friends: Carmen, Lola, and Nerea. Recently the third season of “Valeria” aired on Netflix.

Silma López Early Life & Family

On July 3, 1991, Silma López (age 31) was born to a Moroccan mother and a Spanish father in Madrid, Spain. She has two sisters: Temurá and Kamala. Acording to sources, Silma was a child when her parents got divorced. Her dad moved to Tenerife while her mother stayed in Madrid.

The “Valeria” actress moved a lot in her childhood because her mother and stepfather were actors. There were days when they had plenty of money, but there were also some days when there was a shortage of funds.

She boasts of living in almost every neighborhood of Madrid. Following the footprints of Mom, Silma also chose to become an actress. She began auditioning after high school and soon landed her debut project, “8 Dates,” a romantic movie directed by Peris Romano.

Silma López’s Acting Career

Soon after a banging debut, López landed a part in her first TV show, “Cosas de la vida,” where she played Susi working alongside stars like Ana Mulvoy Ten, Álvaro Monje, and Andreas Muñoz.

Meanwhile, she kept working on theatre productions, keeping her passion for stage performance alive. She also worked on many short movies, including “El Desayuno,” “Una Moneda de Cambio,” and “Son dos días.”

In 2012 she played Camarera in César del Álamo-directed mystery-packed horror film “Good Night, Said Miss Bird.” At 22, she moved to Colombia, where she lived for 2 and a half years and worked on “Cartagena de Indias.” She also fell in love with a Colombian guy there, but the relationship did not last long.

After returning from Colombia, Lopez appeared in a couple more shows, including “Centro médico,” “Private Sales,” and “#Luimelia” but the highlight of her career became the 2020 show “Valeria.”

Who is Silma López Boyfriend?

“Valeria” actress Silma López is unmarried. However, she is in a relationship with a handsome guy named David Rodríguez. Silma and David are not the type of couple who try to hide their personal lives from the public eye.
