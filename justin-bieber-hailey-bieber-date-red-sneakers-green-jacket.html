Justin and Hailey Bieber's Date Night Fits Feature Bold Pops of Color

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, July 4, 2024

It was date night for the Biebers! And not only did they look like they were dressed for the same event, they also each brought a statement pop of color to their ultra-cool fits.

The couple was photographed holding hands leaving a romantic dinner at Italian restaurant Funke in Beverly Hills. While you might have expected Hailey to embody the trending “mob wife aesthetic” for the occasion, she opted for a sleek, Matrix-esque outfit once again—a look that appears to be becoming a favorite of hers. The Rhode founder channeled the science-fiction classic by layering a shiny oversize, ankle-length black leather trench coat over a simple gray sweater and light-wash jeans, paired with—of course—black rectangular shades and gold hoop earrings. Hailey’s footwear also commanded attention: She sported bold, traffic-halting red sneakers. The model finished everything off with a muted red lip and glossy manicure, and held a large but shallow black leather tote bag.

Justin looked just as suave beside his wife in a muted teal-blue button-down, along with flowy black pants, chunky white dad sneakers that featured brown accents throughout, and a black baseball cap, which he wore backward, as is his wont. For his eye-catching pop of color, the “Peaches” singer pulled out an olive-green leather bomber jacket that featured a cozy shearling-lined collar.

The Biebers have been sharing many sweet, heartwarming moments together lately. Earlier this month, Justin put their love front and center when he posted PDA-filled photos from close friend Lori Harvey’s birthday celebration. One of the black-and-white snaps showed the couple holding hands and kissing. A couple weeks before then, Justin shared an image of himself and Hailey cozying up at a Toronto Maple Leafs game.
